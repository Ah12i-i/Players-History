Manchester United (2003–2009)

Transfer Fee: €12 million (2003)

Key Achievements:

  • 3 Premier League titles (2007, 2008, 2009)
  • 1 UEFA Champions League (2008)
  • 1 Ballon d'Or (2008)
  • 1 FIFA Club World Cup (2008)
  • 1 FA Cup (2004)
  • 2 English League Cups (2006, 2009)
  • Ronaldo's time at Manchester United was a transformative period in his career. His incredible performances were key to winning multiple domestic and international titles. He was crucial in securing three Premier League titles, one UEFA Champions League, and a number of domestic cups. His consistency, especially in the 2007-2008 season, where he scored 42 goals, earned him the Ballon d'Or in 2008, cementing his place among the best players in football history. Ronaldo’s work ethic, leadership, and ability to score in crucial moments helped Manchester United dominate English football during his time there.

Real Madrid (2009–2018)

Transfer Fee: €94 million (2009)

Key Achievements:

  • 4 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 2 La Liga titles
  • 4 Ballon d'Ors
  • Real Madrid's all-time top scorer (450 goals)
  • Cristiano Ronaldo's move to Real Madrid in 2009 marked a historic turning point in the club's success. His €94 million transfer fee was a world record at the time, solidifying his status as one of the best players globally. Over the span of nine seasons, Ronaldo's exceptional performances played a pivotal role in Real Madrid's dominance in both European and Spanish football. He achieved remarkable success, securing four UEFA Champions League titles, two La Liga titles, and a staggering four Ballon d'Or awards, further cementing his legacy as one of the greatest footballers of all time. During his tenure, Ronaldo also became Real Madrid's all-time top scorer, netting an incredible 450 goals in official competitions. His contributions helped propel the team to numerous victories, making him a central figure in Real Madrid's era of success.

Juventus (2018–2021)

Transfer Fee: €100 million (2018)

Key Achievements:

  • 2 Serie A titles
  • 1 Coppa Italia title
  • 1 Supercoppa Italiana
  • Serie A MVP (2019)
  • Cristiano Ronaldo’s transfer to Juventus in 2018 was a major milestone in his career. With his immense goal-scoring ability, he became the top scorer for Juventus in every season he played. His presence helped the club maintain its dominance in Italy, winning multiple domestic titles. Additionally, he played a crucial role in strengthening Juventus' European ambitions, delivering exceptional performances in the Champions League. His impact extended beyond the pitch, elevating the club’s global marketability and popularity.

Return to Manchester United (2021–2022)

Key Achievements:

  • Manchester United’s top scorer (2021-22)
  • Premier League Player of the Month (2x)
  • Champions League group stage top scorer
  • Scored a hat-trick against Tottenham
  • Ronaldo’s return to Manchester United in 2021 was highly anticipated, and he quickly made his presence felt by scoring on his debut. Despite Manchester United's struggles, he consistently delivered outstanding performances, finishing as the club's top scorer for the season. His crucial goals in the Champions League helped the team progress beyond the group stage, and he remained one of the Premier League’s top performers. Though his second spell at the club was short, his leadership and experience were invaluable.

Al Nassr (2022–Present)

Key Achievements:

  • First European superstar in Saudi football
  • Scored on his Saudi Pro League debut
  • Won the Arab Club Champions Cup
  • Increased Saudi Pro League’s global reach
  • Ronaldo’s move to Al Nassr in 2022 marked a historic shift in football, as he became the biggest star to play in the Saudi Pro League. His presence attracted worldwide attention and inspired a wave of top players to join the league. Beyond his influence off the field, Ronaldo delivered stellar performances, scoring goals and helping Al Nassr secure titles. His impact is seen in the growing reputation of Saudi football on the international stage.

International Career with Portugal

Debut: 2003

Key Achievements:

  • UEFA Euro 2016 Champion
  • UEFA Nations League 2019 Winner
  • All-time top scorer for Portugal
  • Most international caps for Portugal
  • Since his debut in 2003, Ronaldo has been the backbone of the Portuguese national team. His leadership and ability to perform under pressure led Portugal to its first-ever major title in Euro 2016, followed by another triumph in the UEFA Nations League. With over 120 international goals, he is the all-time top scorer for his country and continues to be a crucial figure in international football.
